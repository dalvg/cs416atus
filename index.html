American Time Use Insights


What changes can be seen in the amount of time people spend on various activities? The visualization below examines data from the American Time Use Survey in three year increments starting from 2015 and ending in 2024.The graph below shows how many hours were spent daily on various activities.

Time can be examined in three-year increments using the buttons. Hovering over each bar provides a tooltip with more details on the amount of time spent on an activity.

Annotations throughout the charts draw attention to certain details, such as how time socializing and communicating has decreased, how women spend significantly more time on tasks such as food preparation and housework, and how men have begun to spend more time on those same tasks throughout the years.
